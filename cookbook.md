# Classic Italian Lasagna


## Ingredients

### For the Meat Sauce

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
- 800 g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the Béchamel Sauce

- 50 g (4 tbsp) butter
- 50 g (1/3 cup) all-purpose flour
- 500 ml (2 cups) milk
- Salt to taste
- A pinch of nutmeg

### Additional Ingredients

- 9–12 lasagna sheets
- 200 g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella
- 50 g (1/2 cup) grated Parmesan


## Instructions

### 1. Prepare the Meat Sauce

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft.
3. Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
5. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste.
6. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
7. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

### 2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add the flour and whisk continuously for 1–2 minutes.
3. Gradually add the milk while whisking to avoid lumps.
4. Cook until the sauce thickens.
5. Season with salt and nutmeg.

### 3. Assemble the Lasagna

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
4. Spread meat sauce on top, then béchamel sauce.
5. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
7. Finish with béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan on top.

### 4. Bake

- Bake for 35–40 minutes until golden brown.
- Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
