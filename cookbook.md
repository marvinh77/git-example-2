# Classic Italian Lasagna


## Ingredients

### For the Meat Sauce

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
- 800 g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the Béchamel Sauce

- 50 g (4 tbsp) butter
- 50 g (1/3 cup) all-purpose flour
- 500 ml (2 cups) milk
- Salt to taste
- A pinch of nutmeg

### Additional Ingredients

- 9–12 lasagna sheets
- 200 g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella
- 50 g (1/2 cup) grated Parmesan
